Soulmates

Art Anidos Ananda 05.12.2019

After meeting my first soulmate some years ago I was sure that there is one for everyone.

Now I have changed my mind.

There are many ❤️

A girlfriend from Denmark told me once. “I had a vision”, she said, a few days after I told her I want to travel to India and want to learn Yoga there. “I have seen you with your soulmate in an ashram.”

Not only that she told me about my next lover, she also was able to let go of our relationship. She is one of them.

Later, after deciding not to travel to India I went to Berlin to meet Joseph Kramer. He is the founder of Sexological Bodywork and I was still in this education. In Berlin I have decided to continue my education after meeting all these great people in Spirit, a place where I held my first workshops a few years later.

A man from Oslo booked a session with me and after the session we were talking about Yoga and Natha-Yoga in Oslo. I told him that I know Natha from Malmö in Sweden and he responded that they are also in Berlin.

The very next day I was at the opening ceremony for Tantra Yoga and I met this beautiful woman this day.

After being a couple for about a few weeks I have realized that we met each other in an ashram ❤️

I finally found my soul mate.

Yesterday me and a good friend in Bern realized that there are additional soulmates all over.

They are there to pull us into a specific direction and might leave right after being useful.

There was a very nice woman who pulled me to Bern a couple of days ago. After I have arrived she immediately fade out of my life. A day later another woman was there for me. I met her 6 years ago. She invited me for a beer on my birthday. This day I met one of those people who read my first book “Die Kunst zu Leben und zu Lieben“ and he invited me into his house.

I stood there for a couple of days. We had a great time. This meeting was very important for both of us.

Also the woman who brought us together faded away. Her job was done.

Some soulmates stay a while and some work like angels and fade away quickly after serving us.

We have to let go…
